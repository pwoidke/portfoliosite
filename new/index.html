About

Paul Woidke is a software developer in Columbus, OH. He has experience in software development with a strong focus on web and mobile development, specifically in developing applications using .NET technologies like C# and SQL, native iOS applications, and web applications using including HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Paul is a graduate of Bowling Green State University, where he received degrees in Digital Art and Computer Science. He has worked as a student volunteer at ACM SIGGRAPH conference, was a member of Kappa Kappa Psi, National Honorary Band Fraternity, and has formal training in fine arts and graphic design.
