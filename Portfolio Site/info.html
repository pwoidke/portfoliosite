About Paul Woidke


Paul Woidke is a recent graduate of Bowling Green State University in Bowling Green, OH, where he received degrees in Digital Art and Computer Science. Originally from Lakewood, OH, Paul attended Lakewood High School where he became involved in both music and visual arts. Upon his acceptance to BGSU, Paul launched into the Bachelor of Fine Art program in Digital Art. In the Spring of 2009 he began an second degree program, this time in the Bachelor of Art program in Computer Science. In addition to his artwork, Paul has been a student volunteer at ACM SIGGRAPH and is an active member in the university band program and member of Kappa Kappa Psi, National Honorary Band Fraternity.
